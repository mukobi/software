Introductions to Dream Work

Introduction to Dream Work

By popular demand, this casual information night gives you a memorable taste of what dream work can do for you. We'll introduce the fundamentals of dream work, and you'll sample exercises that reveal the creative and unconscious forces within you. If you're considering Awaken to Your Dreams, but are curious about our program, this is for you.

Awaken to Your Dreams

Begin your journey with this eye-opening, thought provoking and comprehensive introduction to how and why we dream. Combining Jungian dream theory with dream science and personal dream work, this event is packed with practical information and tools for how to understand your dreaming mind.
