About Dreamwork Training

Dreams are deeply personal for the Dreamer, and have a therapeutic and transformative power when understood. Their energy is intimate, and so dreams leave us feeling vulnerable, exposed. It is no surprise, therefore, that dream work with clients, friends and co-workers is delicate territory. Handled professionally, you can catalyze profound shifts in the dreamer. Handled unconsciously, and even with the best intentions, you can easily do more harm than good. 

Dreamwork Training Days are experiential workshops where you'll learn how to approach other people’s dreams with sensitivity, integrity and care. Professionals will learn valuable techniques for gaining critical therapeutic insight from their clients’ dreams. Experienced dreamers will also have supervised experience working on real dreams, and you’ll receive feedback on your personal style and accuracy. As practice and honest feedback are critical for those doing dream work, both groups will meet together for a practice day a week later, with the option of meeting on an ongoing basis.

About Ilana

Ilana is a psychotherapist in private practice and the founder of Dream Lab, an educational organisation dedicated to the study of dreams.

Ilana writes and teachers Dream Lab's annual education program, as well as engaging local and international speakers to expand our knowledge as a global community. Ilana is a public speaker on the subject of dream intelligence and the language of the unconscious.

Ilana facilitates seminars, dream groups and works privately with clients who have discovered therapeutic dream work.
