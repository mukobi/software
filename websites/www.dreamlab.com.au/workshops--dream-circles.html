Level 2. Individuality & Inner Conflict in Dreams

And Featuring: Music & Myth by David Elias
Carl Jung observed that we are each individuals who have been conditioned by social forces to behave in similar ways. Our individuality is largely hidden, often even from ourselves. In our dreams, however, we naturally express our most subjective, most personal truths. How can we use dreams to understand the inner conflicts we face, and become the individuals we could be? This is a highly practical workshop where we learn to identify the hidden aspects of ourselves represented in dreams, and prepare to integrate them meaningfully into our waking lives.
Prerequisite: Level 1 ~ Learn to Understand Your Dreams
Price: $150

Level 3. Myth, Symbols & Archetypes in Dreams

And Featuring: Music & Myth by David Elias
This is a huge day, where we begin to touch the ultimate application of dream work. Your dreams combine images from your own life with collective symbols from nature, mythology and the history of thought, in order to accurately express who you are. Learn to recognize these symbols and myths, and discover how they have been influencing mankind for thousands of years. This is a deeper level of dream work, where we begin to understand how our personal stories emerge from and contribute to the collective unconscious. 
Prerequisite: Level 1 ~ Learn to Understand Your Dreams
Price: $150

Practice Day: Interpreting the Dreams of Others 

A practice day for Dream Lab members. In this workshop you will learn techniques for safely and respectfully working on each other's dreams. Get supervised experience working on real dreams, and receive feedback on your personal style and accuracy of insight. Practice and honest feedback are critical for those doing dream work. As dreams are deeply personal for the Dreamer, today you'll get a feel for how to approach them with sensitivity, integrity and care. 
Prerequisite: Level 1 & either Level 2 or 3
Location: Centre for Emotion Focused Practice, South Yarra
